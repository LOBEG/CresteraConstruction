Vendor Compliance & Requirements

Approval is conditional and requires ongoing U.S. compliance verification.

This is a public summary only. Full contractor agreement terms are issued upon approval and prior to any work order being awarded.
Required

Mandatory Documents

  • Certificate of Insurance (General Liability, $1M minimum)
  • W-9 submission
  • State contractor/trade license (where required)
  • OSHA compliance confirmation
  • Legal U.S. work authorization
Ongoing

Performance & Conduct

  • Quality workmanship to scope and inspection standards
  • On-time milestone completion and response time
  • OSHA-safe site conduct and PPE adherence
  • Change orders must be pre-approved
  • No work proceeds without a written work order and an approved change order where applicable
  • Ongoing compliance renewal (COI, licensing)

By applying for Approved Contractor Status you confirm that submitted information is accurate and that you can provide supporting evidence upon request.
